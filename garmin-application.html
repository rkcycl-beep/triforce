General Program — Most Important Field

How do you plan to use the Garmin Connect Developer Program?
TriForce is a team sports challenge platform designed for multisport athletes — primarily triathletes, runners, cyclists, and swimmers. The platform enables coaches to create time-bound challenges for their training groups (e.g., weekly distance goals, training load targets, pacing challenges). Athletes connect their Garmin devices to automatically sync activity data, eliminating the need for manual entry. We plan to use both the Activity API and the Health API: • Activity API: To pull completed workout data (running, cycling, swimming) and calculate each athlete's progress toward active challenges. This includes distance, duration, pace, and heart rate zone data. • Health API: To support wellness-based challenges defined by coaches, such as sleep targets, daily step goals, and stress management — metrics that are critical for endurance athletes. A key feature of TriForce is age-graded scoring (Age Grade Score), which normalizes performance across different ages and genders, allowing fair comparison within mixed-age training groups. Garmin data feeds directly into this scoring engine. Initially deployed for a group of approximately 40 athletes in Israel, the platform is designed to scale to multiple independent coaching groups, each with their own coach, athletes, and challenge calendar. Future expansion to other countries is planned.
Do you intend to share data with other platforms?
No. All Garmin activity data is used exclusively within the TriForce platform for the athlete who consented to the connection. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.
