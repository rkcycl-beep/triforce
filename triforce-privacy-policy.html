Privacy Policy

Product: TriForce  |  Last updated: March 2026  |  Contact: robbie@triforce-app.com

TriForce ("we", "our", or "us") is a sports team challenge platform that helps coaches and athletes track training progress, participate in group challenges, and connect their fitness devices. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender, and sport type(s).
  • Fitness activity data: Distance, duration, pace, heart rate, and training load — imported from connected devices (Garmin Connect, Strava) only with your explicit consent.
  • Health metrics (optional): Sleep duration, stress score, and body battery — imported from Garmin Health API only if you choose to connect.
  • Usage data: App interactions, challenge participation, and messaging within the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate your progress toward coach-defined challenges.
  • To display group leaderboards using age-graded scoring (Age Grade Score).
  • To send notifications and coach messages within the app.
  • To improve platform features and performance (anonymized analytics only).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your individual activity data with third parties outside the platform. Activity data is visible to your coach and fellow group members only within your registered team, as part of the challenge and leaderboard features you participate in.

We integrate with Garmin Connect and Strava solely to import your activity data on your behalf, following their respective API terms of service.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and activity data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your personal information at any time within the app.
  • Deletion: You may request full deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdrawal of consent: You may disconnect Garmin or Strava at any time from within the app. This will stop all future data imports immediately.
  • Data portability: You may request an export of your activity data in a standard format.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. OAuth tokens for Garmin and Strava are stored encrypted and are never exposed to other users. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

7. Third-Party Services

TriForce integrates with the following third-party platforms, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Garmin Connect Privacy Policy
  • Strava Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

TriForce is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of any material changes via in-app notification or email.
